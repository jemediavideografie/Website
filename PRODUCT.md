# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Wedding couples in OWL (Ostwestfalen-Lippe) and wider NRW/Lower Saxony, typically 25–40, planning the most significant day of their lives. They browse in the evening, emotionally invested, looking for a videographer they can genuinely trust. They are not shopping for a commodity service; they are looking for the right person. Mobile-first browsing, likely first encounter via Instagram or personal recommendation.

## Product Purpose

JE Media is Joel Enrico Ediger's sole-operator wedding videography business. The website is the primary conversion surface: it must make a couple feel "this is the person I want at my wedding" before they ever write an inquiry. Success means a contact form submitted or a WhatsApp message sent. Secondary goal: communicate credibility and cinematic craft so that price is never the first question.

## Brand Personality

Cinematisch, Emotional, Persönlich. Voice: warm and direct, slightly poetic but never flowery. Joel works alone; the site should feel like a talented person, not an agency. Copy sounds like someone who genuinely loves what they do, not like a marketing pamphlet.

## Anti-references

- Generic videographer aesthetic: black background, yellow lightning bolt, action-hero energy, "we capture moments" in Impact font. Loud, impersonal, transactional.
- Corporate / agency feeling: cold distance, bullet-point "packages", stock imagery, no human presence. Makes the couple feel like a line item.

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't tell.** The video is the argument. Design gets out of the way and lets footage carry emotional weight.
2. **One person, not a company.** Every design decision should signal sole operator: personal, present, unhurried. No "our team", no enterprise grid.
3. **Warmth through restraint.** Overdesign signals insecurity. Confidence is quiet. Elegant simplicity trusts the work.
4. **Cinema lighting, not studio white.** Dark not because it's trendy, but because it creates focus the way a dark room makes a film feel immersive. The site IS the pre-show.
5. **Moments over metrics.** No conversion-rate thinking visible in the UI. No "Book Now" urgency. Couples should feel invited, not pressured.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. German language throughout. Respect prefers-reduced-motion for all animations. High contrast maintained on dark backgrounds; accent gold (#d7ad63 equivalent) used only for decoration and non-essential highlights, never as the sole color carrier for required information.
